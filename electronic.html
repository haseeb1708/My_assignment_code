Computer

A Computer is an electronic device that processes, stores, and retrieves data, performing a wide range of tasks from basic calculations to complex simulations. There are two main types: desktop computers, which are stationary and offer powerful performance, and laptops, which are portable. Computers have revolutionized the way we work, communicate, and entertain ourselves. They consist of hardware components like the processor, memory, storage, and peripherals (keyboard, mouse, monitor). With the rise of the internet and software applications, computers have become indispensable tools in fields like business, education, research, and entertainment, making them a fundamental part of modern life.


Laptop

A Laptop is a portable computer designed for mobile use, combining the functionality of a desktop computer with the convenience of being lightweight and compact. Laptops are equipped with built-in components like a keyboard, screen, touchpad, and battery, making them ideal for students, professionals, and travelers. They come in various sizes and configurations, offering different levels of performance based on the user's needs. Laptops are perfect for multitasking, online browsing, gaming, and even complex tasks like video editing or coding. With advancements in technology, modern laptops are becoming more powerful, with longer battery life and sleeker designs, allowing users to work or entertain themselves from anywhere.
